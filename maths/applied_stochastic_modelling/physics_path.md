# Physics Learning Path (Aligned with Stochastic Modelling)

Purpose: build physics intuition that complements probability, stochastic processes, and complex systems.

## Stage 1 — Conceptual Mechanics
**Book**
- Leonard Susskind — *The Theoretical Minimum: Classical Mechanics*

Focus
- energy
- forces
- motion
- state of a system

---

## Stage 2 — Problem‑Solving Mechanics
**Book**
- David Morin — *Introduction to Classical Mechanics*
  (alternative: John R. Taylor — *Classical Mechanics*)

Focus
- modelling physical systems
- conservation laws
- translating real problems into equations

---

## Stage 3 — Thermal Physics
**Book**
- Daniel Schroeder — *Thermal Physics*

Focus
- entropy
- probability in physical systems
- microscopic vs macroscopic behaviour

---

## Stage 4 — Statistical Mechanics & Complex Systems
**Book**
- James Sethna — *Statistical Mechanics: Entropy, Order Parameters and Complexity*

Focus
- random walks
- diffusion
- phase transitions
- emergent behaviour

---

## Stage 5 — Networked Systems
**Book**
- Albert‑László Barabási — *Network Science* (free online)

Focus
- network structure
- cascading failures
- robustness of infrastructure networks

---

## Stage 6 — Stochastic Physics (Advanced)
**Books**
- C. W. Gardiner — *Stochastic Methods*
- N. G. Van Kampen — *Stochastic Processes in Physics and Chemistry*

Focus
- Brownian motion
- stochastic differential equations
- diffusion models
