# Biology Learning Path (Systems & Quantitative Focus)

Purpose: understand biological systems using modelling, probability, and complex‑systems thinking.

## Stage 1 — Big Picture Biology
**Book**
- Nick Lane — *Life Ascending*

Focus
- major biological innovations
- evolution of complex life
- energetic foundations of biology

---

## Stage 2 — Molecular Foundations
**Book**
- Alberts et al. — *Molecular Biology of the Cell* (selected reading)

Focus
- cells
- proteins
- DNA and gene expression
- cellular systems

---

## Stage 3 — Systems Biology
**Book**
- Uri Alon — *An Introduction to Systems Biology*

Focus
- network motifs
- regulatory circuits
- feedback loops in biology

---

## Stage 4 — Evolutionary Dynamics
**Book**
- Martin Nowak — *Evolutionary Dynamics*

Focus
- population models
- evolutionary game theory
- stochastic evolution

---

## Stage 5 — Quantitative / Mathematical Biology
**Book**
- Iain Couzin & Simon Levin — *The Princeton Guide to Ecology* (selected topics)
  or
- Leah Edelstein‑Keshet — *Mathematical Models in Biology*

Focus
- population dynamics
- ecological systems
- pattern formation
- biological networks
